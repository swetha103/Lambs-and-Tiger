UNITY IS STRENGTH

The game is alternately played by Goat and Tiger players, always started by the Goat player.

The game ends when either all the Tigers or Goats are out of moves to proceed further.

Initially

Tigers have a fixed position and can move. They are always 3 in number. And do not get killed.

15 Goats can only be placed and can be moved once all the Goats have come into the game.

A Tiger can kill a Goat by jumping over it.
That is called a Match move.

Movement is only possible if it is connected.
